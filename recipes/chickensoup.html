Delicious Chicken Soup

Perfect for the days when you feel sick and you need a reconfortant meal

  • Chicken: Add chicken pieces according to the amount of people that you will feed, one big chicken breast its also a good option
  • Vegetables Carrot and peas are the best option for this soup
  • Potato 3 potatoes cutted in small cubes
  • Noodles Adding noodels to this soup will give it a nice touch, you can use the noodels from an instant soup

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a large pot over medium heat. Add onion and celery and cook until just tender, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add chicken broth, vegetable broth, chicken, egg noodles, carrots, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine and bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes.
